    Student's Life


          Thammasat University Pattaya Center have many modern facilities, sufficient classrooms to meet the needs of students. There is a restaurant for student prices. If a restaurant close, there has a mini-mart within walking distance to shop. There is also a dormitory to supports the students who they home is far away. The university has prepared a place for students for study.Also, the university have organized various activities that teach high school students. The activities to be interconnected relationships between sectors and also a Volunteer activities for students to use their free time for the benefit.
